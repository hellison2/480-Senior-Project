Welcome to "The Best Coffee In Town"

We hope to help you discover new coffee shops and cafes in the Phoenix metro area, with an emphasis on cafes that are conducive to studying or remote work. You can use our map to navigate to the cafes nearest to you or use our Listings page to see all the options in one place.

Once you’ve found a cafe you like, you can click on the listing to see a full review of the coffee shop, including important metrics such as availability of wifi or food, and opening hours. We’ll give you our personal take on each coffee shop on the list in hopes of helping you choose the best fit for you.

About Our Site

The Best Coffee In Town was created to help students and remote workers find the best places in Phoenix to get some work done at!

There are hundreds of cafes in the area, but between early closing times, limited seating, and the availability of wifi or food, it can be difficult to find the perfect cafe for all your needs, nevermind one that has the right atmosphere or outlets available to work at.

The goal of this website is to provide some fail-proof options to get high-quality lattes and focus on your work for as long as you wish, and of course, prioritize shops that are open late for fellow late risers.
